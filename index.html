About islamfactcheck.org

www.islamfactcheck.org was created to provide quick access to verifiable, fact-based information so that people of all faiths can use this information proactively in everything from presentations and class assignments to sermons, as well as to respond to falsehoods promoted by anti-Muslim hate groups about Islamic beliefs and practices and Muslims’ lives, contributions, hopes and dreams. All facts cited in this web site are hyperlinked to source information for easy verification.
